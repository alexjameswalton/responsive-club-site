The Bangalore Piano Scene

Bangalore is a melting pot, where modern meets traditional, and classical beats up against fusion in every walk of life. The music scene is no exception. Some of the countries hottest musicians ply their wears here, with influencers from Karnatic through Jazz&Blues, to A.R.Rahman and Neo Soul. These guys have it all!
